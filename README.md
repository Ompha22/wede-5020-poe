## Project Overview

Kefi’s Catering is a food business that was started in 2022 by Kefi, driven by a love for cooking and a dream to share tasty, well-prepared meals with people. It began as a small home-based service and slowly grew as more customers enjoyed the food and recommended it to others. Today, Kefi’s Catering provides catering for different events like weddings, birthdays, and corporate functions, always focusing on fresh ingredients, beautiful presentation, and reliable service. Since it started, the business has built a good reputation and continues to grow by creating memorable food experiences for every occasion.

## Website Goals and Objectives

The goal of Kefi’s Catering website is to create a welcoming online space where people can easily learn about the brand, explore the services offered, and feel confident choosing it for their events. It should attract new customers while making it simple for them to get in touch or place orders. The website will clearly show menus and services, highlight past events and customer experiences, and encourage more inquiries and bookings, all while being easy to use, visually appealing, and accessible on any device.

## Timeline and Milestones

In Week 1, the planning phase will focus on defining the website goals and objectives and gathering all necessary content such as menus, images, and business information. In Week 2, the design phase will involve creating the sitemap and planning the layout of the website, including the homepage and main pages. Week 3 will be dedicated to development, where the website will be built using HTML and CSS and all content will be added. In Week 4, testing will take place to identify and fix any errors, as well as ensure the website works well on different devices, especially mobile. Finally, in Week 5, the website will be launched and promoted online to attract customers.
